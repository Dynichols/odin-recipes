Homemade Lasagna

Description

The following recipe will showcase how to make a homemade lasagna with a tomato and meat sauce and a cheese filling. It substitutes cottage cheese and parmesan for traditional ricotta.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground pork
  • 1/2 lb ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 (8 oz) can of tomato sauce
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsps parsley
  • 1 clove crushed garlic
  • 1 1/2 tsps basil
  • 1 1/2 tsps salt
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/8 tsp sugar
  • 1 (16oz) pack of lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb cottage cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tsps salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 (16 oz) package shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Combine pork and ground beef in large skillet over medium-high heat, cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5-7 minutes. Add onino and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes and tomato sauce, and herbs and seasonings, reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionaly for 30 minutes
  3. While sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions, stirring occasionally, drain and set aside.
  4. Preheat oven to 375 degrees Fareinheit, or 190 degrees celsius
  5. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remianing parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  6. Add a thin layer of sauce to bottom of 9x13" baking dish, layer noodles with 1/2 of the sauce and cheese, and 1/2 the mozzarella. Alternate and cover with foil.
  7. Bake in oven for 30-40 minutes. Remove foil and bake until golden brown, 5-10 more minutes.
  8. Remove from oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
